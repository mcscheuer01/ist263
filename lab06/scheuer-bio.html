Molly's Biography

My name is Molly Scheuer, and I am a senior at Syracuse University in the School of Information Studies. I am studying Information Management and Technology with concentrations in Data Analytics and Digital Retail Strategies, and hope to go into data analytics in the music or fashion industry.
